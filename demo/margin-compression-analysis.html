Financial & Management Reporting

Automates financial and management reporting by consolidating data across business units and generating real-time reports without manual intervention. Delivers executive-ready outputs with AI-generated commentary.

Role in Workflow

Consolidates business unit data, generates variance commentary identifying where margins are compressing, and produces the final executive report with KPI dashboard.

Pricing Analytics

Provides cognitive pricing analytics and pricing automation capabilities. Analyzes market dynamics, competitor pricing, customer behavior, and cost structures to recommend optimal pricing strategies that maximize revenue.

Role in Workflow

Benchmarks pricing against competitors, analyzes revenue-side erosion, and simulates the revenue impact of corrective pricing adjustments.

Procure-to-Pay

Helps procurement teams process supplier invoices quickly and accurately. Validates invoices against purchase orders and contracts, flags exceptions that need human review, and optimizes payment workflows for discount capture.

Role in Workflow

Vets supplier pricing competitiveness, validates invoice costs against purchase orders, and identifies supplier renegotiation and cost optimization opportunities.

Financial Forecasting

Helps FP&A professionals build accurate cash flow projections by analyzing historical financial data, simulating multiple business scenarios, and identifying potential budget variances before they occur.

Role in Workflow

Runs scenario simulations projecting the combined impact of pricing changes and cost actions on margin recovery over 12 months.
